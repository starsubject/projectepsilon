How to Get Your Scratch Token

Instructions

Follow the steps below to get your Scratch token. This is for your own Scratch account and is safe as long as you keep it private. You can use this token to interact with the Scratch API, but never share it with anyone else.

  1. Open your Scratch profile page in your browser.
  2. Open the Developer Tools in your browser (Right-click > Inspect or press F12).
  3. Go to the Network tab in the Developer Tools.
  4. In the search bar, type your username to filter the network requests.
  5. Look for a request with your username, then right-click on it.
  6. Hover over Copy and then click on Copy as Powershell.
  7. Paste the copied Powershell script into the input field below.
